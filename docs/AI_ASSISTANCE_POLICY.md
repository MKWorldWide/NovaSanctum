# NovaSanctum AI Assistance Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-07

## 1. Scope

This policy defines acceptable AI use in NovaSanctum learner experiences and internal program operations.

## 2. Approved AI Functions

AI may be used to:

1. Clarify concepts from cited source materials.
2. Suggest next steps based on roadmap progress.
3. Support pacing plans and study scheduling.
4. Provide plain-language explanations and summaries.
5. Surface relevant open resources based on learner goals.

## 3. Prohibited AI Functions

AI may not be used to:

1. Act as instructor-of-record.
2. Issue credentials, grades, or formal academic determinations.
3. Present uncited claims as authoritative facts.
4. Replace source attribution with model-generated assertions.
5. Make high-stakes decisions without human oversight.

## 4. Transparency Requirements

1. AI-assisted responses should include source references where applicable.
2. Users should be informed when content is AI-assisted.
3. Limitations and uncertainty should be disclosed in plain language.

## 5. Human Oversight

1. Program staff retain responsibility for policy, curation, and quality standards.
2. AI outputs used in public-facing materials are subject to human review.
3. Safety and quality issues are triaged and corrected through documented workflows.

## 6. Data and Privacy

1. Collect the minimum data required for learner support.
2. Do not expose sensitive personal data to third-party models without explicit safeguards.
3. Retain logs only as needed for security, quality, and compliance.

## 7. Continuous Review

This policy is reviewed quarterly during active development and at least annually after stabilization.
