# NovaSanctum Mission Charter

Effective date: 2026-02-07  
Program: NovaSanctum  
Organization context: MK Worldwide (nonprofit)

## 1. Purpose

NovaSanctum improves access to education by curating free, open-access learning resources and organizing them into structured, guided pathways for self-directed learners.

## 2. Mission Commitments

NovaSanctum will:

1. Curate publicly accessible educational materials with clear source attribution.
2. Build sequential learning roadmaps with transparent prerequisites and outcomes.
3. Use AI as assistive guidance for navigation, pacing, and explanation.
4. Prioritize inclusion, accessibility, and practical learner outcomes.

## 3. Institutional Position

NovaSanctum is complementary to formal education and does not claim to replace accredited schools, universities, instructors, or credentialing systems.

## 4. AI Position

AI systems in NovaSanctum are support tools. They are not instructional authorities, final arbiters of correctness, or credential issuers.

## 5. Content Position

NovaSanctum does not claim ownership of third-party educational resources. The program provides curation, pathway structure, and learner support around externally sourced content.

## 6. Governance Principles

1. Transparency in curation and source selection.
2. Ethical conservatism in AI deployment.
3. Accountability for accuracy, safety, and clarity.
4. Incremental implementation and measurable improvement.

## 7. Review Cycle

This charter is reviewed at least annually or when major program scope changes occur.
